# NTT DATA - Associate Security Platform Engineer

## I. Interview Experience

The recruitment process for Security Platform roles at NTT DATA focuses heavily on the intersection of infrastructure, cloud security, and networking.

#### 1. Round 1: Online Assessment (Aptitude & Technical)
- **Aptitude**: Quantitative, Logical Reasoning, and Verbal Ability.
- **Technical MCQs**: Focus on Networking (OSI Model, TCP/IP), Basic Security concepts (Encryption, SSL/TLS), and Cloud Fundamentals.

#### 2. Round 2: Technical Interview 1 (Foundations & Implementation)
- **Networking**: In-depth questions on DNS, HTTP/S, and VPN implementation.
- **Security**: Understanding of CIA triad, Firewalls (Stateful vs. Stateless), and IDS/IPS.
- **Firewalls**: Port-level filtering and common ports (e.g., 22, 80, 443, 3389).

#### 3. Round 3: Technical Interview 2 (Architecture & Scenarios)
- **Cloud Architecture**: Designing secure VPCs/VNets, IAM roles, and Security Groups.
- **Platform Security**: How to secure a Kubernetes cluster (RBAC, Network Policies) and container security.
- **Implementation Scenarios**: "How would you design a secure environment for a client migrating to the cloud?" or "Explain how you managed server security for UPI/BHIM projects."

#### 4. Round 4: HR / Managerial Round
- **Cultural Fit**: Discussion on work ethics, night shift flexibility (US/AU clients), and career goals.

# NTT DATA Security Platform Engineer Interview Experience

The recruitment process at **NTT DATA** for security infrastructure is technically rigorous, shifting away from generalities to focus on deep protocol analysis and "lab-style" scenarios. Candidates must demonstrate a granular understanding of how data moves across a secured network.

---

## Key Aspects of the Interview Experience

### **1. Packet-Level Technical Deep-Dive**
* **Headers & Flow**: Be prepared to explain the internal structure of an **IP Header** and track the **IP Packet Flow** across different OSI layers.
* **Switching & Routing**: Deep focus on **STP (Spanning Tree Protocol)**, **VTP**, and **VLANs**. You must understand the difference between **Static, RIP, OSPF, and BGP** routing, including their specific **AD (Administrative Distance)** values.
* **Security & VPNs**: Questions frequently cover **Access Control Lists (ACL)**, **NAT (Network Address Translation)**, and the configuration/troubleshooting of **Site-to-Site VPNs**.

### **2. Network Services & Management**
* **Infrastructure Services**: Mastery of **DNS, DHCP, SSL**, and **ARP** is required. You should know common **Port Numbers** by heart.
* **Firewall Operations**: Beyond simple rules, they ask about **Backup and Restoration of configurations** on the firewall and how to implement **QoS (Quality of Service)**.
* **Wireless Architecture**: Understanding **WLAN Architecture** (Controllers, APs, and LWAPP) is a common requirement.

### **3. Behavioral & Company Alignment**
* **Cultural Fit**: The final round focuses on your knowledge of **NTT DATA's global presence** and your ability to handle the high-pressure environment of security operations.

---

## Interview Rounds Summary
1.  **Tech HR (The Gauntlet)**: An intensive session covering the entire networking stack, from MAC addresses to BGP and Firewall management.
2.  **Final HR**: Behavioral assessment, self-introduction, and alignment with NTT DATA's core values.
