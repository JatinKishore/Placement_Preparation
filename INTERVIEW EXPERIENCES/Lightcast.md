# Lightcast Interview Experience

The recruitment process at **Lightcast** is comprehensive, evaluating a candidate's ability to balance low-level coding logic with high-level architectural thinking. As a data-centric company, they prioritize candidates who understand how to build scalable and visually consistent design systems.

---

## Key Aspects of the Interview Experience

### **1. Initial Screening & Design Awareness**
* **Aptitude & Logic**: A foundational round testing quantitative ability and logical reasoning.
* **Coding Easy**: Typically involves a straightforward algorithmic challenge (e.g., Array manipulation or String processing).
* **Design Systems**: A unique component of the Lightcast process. You may be asked about component reusability, CSS architecture, or maintaining visual consistency in data-heavy dashboards.

### **2. Collaborative & Communication Filter**
* **Group Discussion (GD)**: Used to assess clarity of thought and the ability to articulate technical or social viewpoints within a team setting.

### **3. Technical Deep-Dives**
* **Round 1 (Resume Focus)**: A 1-hour session strictly following the technologies and skills listed on your resume. Be prepared for deep-dives into your primary tech stack (e.g., React, Python, or AWS).
* **Round 2 (Project & Live Coding)**: An intensive round focusing on the **"Why"** behind your project decisions. Expect to live-code more complex scenarios or optimize existing logic under the interviewer's guidance.

### **4. Personal HR**
* The final round focuses on behavioral fit, career alignment, and understanding Lightcast's mission to drive economic growth through labor market data.

---

## Interview Rounds Summary
1.  **Aptitude & Design Screening**: Quants, Logic, Easy Coding, and Design System fundamentals.
2.  **Group Discussion**: Collaborative evaluation of communication skills.
3.  **Technical Round 1**: 60-minute resume-based technical interrogation.
4.  **Technical Round 2**: Detailed project discussion and medium-level coding.
5.  **Personal HR**: Behavioral assessment and salary finalization.
