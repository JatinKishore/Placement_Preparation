# Petrocil (Guindy)

## I. Petrocil Interview Experience

The recruitment process at Petrocil is focused on core Electrical Engineering fundamentals. The written test is a significant filter, followed by a technical discussion based on your academic background.

#### 1. Written Test (50 MCQs)
Below are the detailed technical questions and answers from the assessment:

**A. Basic Electrical Engineering**
* **Q: The SI unit of power is?**
  * **A**: Watt
* **Q: Electrical pressure is also called?**
  * **A**: Voltage
* **Q: The property of a conductor due to which it passes current is called?**
  * **A**: Conductance
* **Q: The resistance of a conductor varies inversely as?**
  * **A**: Area of cross-section
* **Q: With rise in temperature, the resistance of pure metals?**
  * **A**: Increases

**B. Transformers**
* **Q: The efficiency of a transformer will be maximum when?**
  * **A**: Copper loss = Iron loss
* **Q: Which of the following does not change for a transformer?**
  * **A**: Frequency
* **Q: The transformer core is laminated to?**
  * **A**: Reduce eddy current loss
* **Q: Efficiency of a power transformer is of the order of?**
  * **A**: 98%

**C. Induction Motors**
* **Q: The crawling in the induction motor is caused by?**
  * **A**: Harmonics developed in the motor
* **Q: If any two phases of an induction motor are interchanged?**
  * **A**: The motor will run in reverse direction
* **Q: The frame of an induction motor is usually made up of?**
  * **A**: Cast iron
* **Q: In squirrel cage induction motors, the rotor slots are usually given a slight skew in order to?**
  * **A**: Reduce magnetic hum
