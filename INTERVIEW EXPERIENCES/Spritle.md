# Spritle Software Interview Experience

The recruitment process at **Spritle Software** is streamlined and highly personalized, focusing on a candidate's "builder" mindset and communication clarity. For technical roles, the process moves quickly from initial screening to hands-on evaluation.

---

## Key Aspects of the Interview Experience

### **1. Rapid & Personalized Process**
* **Average Timeline**: The entire process, from application to offer, typically concludes within **11 days**.
* **Direct Interaction**: Interviews are largely one-on-one, allowing for a deep-dive into your personal coding style and problem-solving logic.

### **2. Practical Technical Evaluation**
* **Coding Assessment**: For developers, expect challenges in **JavaScript** (string/array manipulation) or role-specific stacks like **Ruby on Rails**. 
* **Business Analysis (BA) Tasks**: BA candidates often face a unique first round: creating **SRS (Software Requirements Specification)** documentation for a given use case.
* **Architecture Discussion**: After coding, be prepared to discuss **System Architecture Diagrams** and how your solution would scale or deploy via APIs.

### **3. Culture & "Happiness" Fit**
* **Transparency**: Spritle values transparency and a "family" atmosphere. They look for individuals who can work autonomously but communicate effectively within a team.
* **Web3 & AI Focus**: Currently (2025-2026), demonstrating curiosity or experience in **Web3**, **Multimodal AI Agents**, or **Blockchain** is a major differentiator during the technical discussion.

---

## Interview Rounds Summary
1.  **Phone Screening**: Brief HR call to verify resume details and assess communication fluency.
2.  **Technical Assessment**: 
    - *Developers*: Live coding test focusing on fundamentals.
    - *BAs*: Documentation task (SRS/WBS drafting).
3.  **Technical Discussion**: F2F or video round reviewing your task and deep-diving into API/App deployment logic.
4.  **HR & Negotiation**: Final round with the "Culture & Happiness" team to discuss salary, contract terms, and cultural alignment.
