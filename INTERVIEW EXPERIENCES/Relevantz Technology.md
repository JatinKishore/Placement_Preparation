# Relevantz Technology Services Interview Experience

---

## Key Aspects of the Interview Experience

### **1. Domain-Centric Evaluation**
* **Application Over Theory**: Technical rounds prioritize how a candidate uses their primary technology stack to solve **real-world problems**.
* **No Generic Aptitude**: For experienced professionals, the company typically skips logical reasoning or problem-solving puzzles to focus strictly on **professional expertise**.
* **Foundational Strength**: Candidates are expected to have a rock-solid grasp of the **basics of their specific domain** before moving to higher levels.

### **2. Progressive Technical Depth**
* **Technical Round 1**: Primarily covers foundational domain knowledge and practical technology application.
* **Technical Round 2**: Shifts toward **advanced concepts** and architectural understanding within the candidate's core technology.

### **3. HR & Managerial Fit**
* **Conversational Tone**: The HR round is typically a general conversation rather than a high-pressure interview.
* **Personal & Managerial Focus**: Questions focus on **managerial scenarios**, team collaboration, and personal career goals.
