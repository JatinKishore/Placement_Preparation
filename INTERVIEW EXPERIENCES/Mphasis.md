# Mphasis

## I. Mphasis Interview Experience

Mphasis follows a structured recruitment process, often utilizing the AMCAT platform for initial screening. For experienced roles, the focus shifts heavily toward domain expertise and architectural understanding.

#### 1. Online Assessment (AMCAT/SVAR)
- **Aptitude**: Quantitative, logical, and verbal reasoning sections.
- **Technical MCQ**: Focus on Data Structures, OS, and DBMS.
- **SVAR Test**: An automated tool to assess speech, pronunciation, and listening skills.

#### 2. Technical Interview
- **Programming & OOPS**:
    - **Collections**: Internal working of `HashMap` and `ConcurrentHashMap`.
    - **Java 8+**: Extensive questions on Streams and Lambda expressions.
    - **SOLID Principles**: Practical application of design patterns in projects.
- **Cloud & DevOps**:
    - Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using **Terraform**.
    - Containerization logic with **Docker** and CI/CD pipeline security (Snyk/SonarQube).
- **Data Structures**: Linked Lists, Trees, and sorting algorithms.

#### 3. HR Interview
- **Focus**: Cultural alignment, salary expectations, and motivation.
- **Policy**: Discussion on the 24-month training agreement/bond if applicable to the specific role.

## II. Work Experience (Software Engineer)

* **Average Salary**: For experienced roles (3-5 years), packages range from **₹10 LPA to ₹18 LPA**.
* **Pros**: 
    * Strong global presence with opportunities in fintech and banking domains.
    * Structured career growth for Senior Software Engineers (Average ₹9.1 LPA base).
* **Cons**: 
    * Training agreements/bonds may apply for certain specialized entry tracks.
