# ERP Roots Interview Experience

The hiring process at **ERP Roots** focuses on identifying SAP specialists who can manage full implementation lifecycles. It moves from foundational screening to a unique C-Suite interaction, ensuring both technical and cultural alignment.

---

## Key Aspects of the Interview Experience

### **1. SAP Domain Expertise**
* **Module Deep-Dive**: Expect rigorous questions on your specific SAP module (e.g., SAP FICO, SAP MM). Focus on configuration logic and integration points.
* **Implementation Lifecycle**: Be prepared to discuss your experience with the **ASAP methodology**â€”from requirement gathering to Go-Live and support.

### **2. Technical & Problem Solving**
* **Aptitude & Logic**: For many roles, an initial aptitude test evaluates problem-solving speed and logical reasoning.
* **Practical Scenarios**: You may be asked to provide **writing samples** or solve technical scenarios related to business process gaps in SAP.

### **3. C-Suite Interaction**
* **Strategic Alignment**: A unique final stage often involves an in-person interview with the **C-suite (CEO/CTO)**. This round assesses your career goals and how you translate technical SAP features into business ROI.

---

## Interview Rounds Summary
1.  **Initial Screening**: Resume review and skill mapping.
2.  **Aptitude/Writing Test**: Assessment of basic skills and professional documentation.
3.  **Technical Interview**: Deep-dive into SAP modules and resume-based projects.
4.  **HR Interview**: Personality assessment and cultural fitment.
5.  **C-Suite Interview**: In-person strategic discussion with senior leadership.
