# Infosys

## I. Infosys Interview Experience

The Infosys selection process is heavily weighted toward the initial assessment. Shortlisted candidates have a very high conversion rate (approx. 90%) in the final interview.

#### 1. Aptitude Test (7 Sections)
- **Section 1**: Reasoning Ability
- **Section 2**: Technical Ability
- **Section 3**: Verbal Ability
- **Section 4**: Pseudo Code (Logic-based snippets)
- **Section 5**: Puzzles (Logical deduction)
- **Section 6**: Grammar
- **Section 7**: Essay Writing 
    - *Note: Non-scored but mandatory; used for communication and behavioral assessment.*

#### 2. Technical & HR Interview
- **Communication Focus**: The interview is primarily a check for communication clarity and basic technical fluency.
- **Core Coding**: 
    - **Bubble Sort**: Implementation and complexity analysis.
    - [Click & Solve: Bubble Sort Algorithm](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/bubble-sort-algorithm-binary-search-java-queues-stacks-linked-list/)
- **Logic**: Solving a real-time puzzle during the interaction.
- **Tech Stack Advantage**: Candidates with knowledge in **AI/Cloud** concepts have a significantly higher hiring probability.

# Infosys Systems Engineer (SE) Interview Experience

The **Infosys Systems Engineer** recruitment process for the 2026 batch is an assessment of logical clarity, algorithmic thinking, and professional communication. The process is designed to filter candidates who possess strong foundational knowledge in core CS subjects and can solve unconventional problems under time pressure.

---

## Key Aspects of the Interview Experience

### **1. Multi-Sectional Online Assessment**
* **Technical & Logical Breadth**: The test is divided into 5–7 sections, including **Mathematical Ability**, **Analytical Reasoning**, and **Pseudocode**.
* **New Sections**: For the 2026 cycle, sections like **English Grammar** and **English Writing** (essays or formal emails) have been emphasized to ensure global communication standards.
* **Puzzle Solving**: A critical filter involving 4 complex logic puzzles to be solved in roughly 10 minutes.

### **2. Technical Fundamentals**
* **Core CS Subjects**: Expect deep-dives into **OOPS**, **DBMS (SQL subset like DDL/DML)**, and **Software Engineering models** (Waterfall vs. Agile).
* **Programming Logic**: While language-independent, the technical interview often tests your favorite language (Java, C++, or Python) through logic-building questions like Palindrome checks or Matrix manipulations.

### **3. Behavioral & Cultural Fit**
* **Company Values**: Infosys values its heritage and founders' stories. Being aware of the current CEO (**Salil Parekh**) and their leadership in **AI and Cloud** is an advantage.
* **Relocation & Flexibility**: HR rounds frequently confirm your willingness to work in any of their global delivery centers and your comfort with shift-based work.

---

## Interview Rounds Summary
1. **Online Assessment**: 100-minute test covering Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal, Pseudocode, and Puzzles.
2. **Technical + HR Interview**: A 30–45 minute combined session focusing on resume projects, core tech concepts, and behavioral fitment.
