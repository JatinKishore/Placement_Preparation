# Delta Electronics Interview Experience

The recruitment process at **Delta Electronics** is technical and domain-specific, often focusing on the intersection of software engineering, hardware integration, and big data. The company values candidates who can bridge the gap between traditional power electronics and modern AI-driven energy solutions.

---

## Key Aspects of the Interview Experience

### **1. Domain-Specific Screening**
* **Technical Breadth**: Initial conversations often cover a wide range of topics including **IoT, API knowledge**, and hardware-software interfacing.
* **Electronics Fundamentals**: For many roles, be prepared for questions on voltage, resistance, and circuit components, reflecting Delta's heritage in power electronics.

### **2. Data & Big Data Proficiency**
* **Data Engineering**: Interviews for software roles frequently delve into **Big Data** concepts like **Spark, ETL vs. ELT**, and the "4 Vs" of data.
* **SQL & Modeling**: Expect deep dives into **SQL joins, Window functions**, and database schemas (Star vs. Snowflake).

### **3. Coding & Logic**
* **Manual Coding**: Delta often asks candidates to write code for basic problems on paper or a shared document, focusing on **time complexity** and optimization.
* **DSA Focus**: Core data structures like Arrays, Linked Lists, and Hash Maps are staples of the technical assessment.

### **4. Cultural & HR Alignment**
* **Corporate Values**: The "Together" philosophy and a focus on **Innovation and Integrity** are central to their cultural assessment.

## Interview Rounds Summary
1.  **Technical Screening**: Phone screen covering background, SQL, and API basics.
2.  **Technical Round(s)**: 2-3 rounds of coding (DSA), Big Data frameworks (Spark), and Data Modeling.
3.  **Managerial Round**: Project architecture walkthrough and role-specific scenario discussions.
4.  **HR Interview**: Behavioral assessment based on corporate values and final salary negotiation.
